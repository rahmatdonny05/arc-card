Got Questions

FAQ

Arc Cards is a community initiative by Domith celebrating builders in the Arc ecosystem. Each card is a soulbound NFT — permanently tied to your wallet — with a unique visual generated from your username. The card evolves visually as your onchain activity grows: more transactions, higher tier, richer visuals.

Click Connect Wallet in the top right. Works with MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, and any EVM wallet. Arc Testnet (Chain ID 5042002) is added automatically — you don't need to configure it manually.

Your card reads data directly from Arc Testnet via RPC — your transaction count, gas spent, USDC held, and NFTs minted all contribute to a score. Higher score = higher tier: Common → Rare → Epic → Legendary. Each tier unlocks richer visual elements on the card. When you reach a new tier, you can re-mint to lock it permanently onchain.

No. Arc Cards are soulbound — once minted to your wallet they cannot be transferred, sold, or traded. This ensures your card is genuine proof of your presence in the Arc ecosystem, not something that can be bought.

When you reach a new tier (Rare, Epic, Legendary), you can burn your current card and re-mint a new one with the evolved tier locked permanently in the NFT metadata. Common → Rare is free. Rare → Epic costs 3 USDC. Epic → Legendary costs 8 USDC. Legendary re-mint costs 20 USDC.
